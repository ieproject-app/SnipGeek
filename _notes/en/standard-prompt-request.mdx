To ensure that every article is published exactly as you envision, please use the following template for your requests. This checklist helps us align on all the critical details upfront, preventing any omissions.

### Publication Request Checklist

Please include these details in your prompt:

- **Article Type**: Is it a 'blog post' or a 'note'?
- **Title**: What is the title of the article?
- **Content**: Provide the full content for the article.
- **Publication Date**: What is the desired publication date? (e.g., 'February 16, 2026')
- **Translation Key**: Provide a unique key to link this article with its translations (e.g., 'my-awesome-article-v1').
- **Status**: Confirm with 'Set `published: true`'.
- **Image Placeholders**: Mention where to put `<!-- IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER -->` for screenshots.
- **Languages**: Specify all languages for the article (e.g., 'English and Indonesian').

---

### Example Prompt

> Please publish a new **note** for me.
>
> - **Article Type**: Note
> - **Title**: My New Note About Collaboration
> - **Content**: [Your full article content goes here...]
> - **Publication Date**: February 17, 2026
> - **Translation Key**: my-new-note-2026
> - **Status**: Please set `published: true`.
> - **Image Placeholders**: Place one after the second paragraph.
> - **Languages**: English and Indonesian.

By providing these details, you help me execute your vision perfectly on the first try. Thank you for your collaboration!
