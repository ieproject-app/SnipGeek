Whenever you want me to build a new feature, fix a bug, or make changes to the app, please use the format below. The goal is to ensure I have all the details I need, so the final result matches your expectations and no steps are missed.

### 1. Main Goal
*(Required)*

Briefly describe (in one or two sentences) what you ultimately want to achieve.

**Example:**
> "I want to add a 'Reading List' feature so users can save articles to read later."

### 2. Change Details
*(Required)*

This is the most important part. Provide as much detail as possible.

- **Feature/Bug Description:** Explain how you want the feature to work or describe the problem/bug you've found.
- **UI Location:** On which page or in which component should this change appear? (e.g., "in the header," "under the article title," "on all article cards on the homepage").
- **Look & Feel:** Are there any design preferences? (e.g., "use a `Card` component," "center the button," "the icon should be a `Bookmark`").
- **User Flow:** Describe the steps a user would take when interacting with this feature.

**Example:**
> - **Description:** A button to save an article to a reading list. When clicked, the article is saved to `localStorage`, and the button's state changes.
> - **UI Location:** Appears on the article detail page and also on every article card on the homepage.
> - **Look & Feel:** Use a `Bookmark` icon. If saved, the icon becomes solid.
> - **Flow:** User clicks the icon -> icon changes -> a "Article saved" notification appears.

### 3. Image/Asset Placement
*(Optional)*

If you want me to insert a placeholder for an image you'll add later (like a screenshot), just let me know where.

**Example:**
> "Under the 'User Flow' section, please insert a placeholder for an image."

{/* You can place your image/screenshot here */}

### 4. Things to Avoid
*(Optional)*

Are there any constraints or things I should not do?

**Example:**
> "Do not change the `header`'s background color."

By following this format, we can minimize miscommunication and speed up the development process. Thank you for your cooperation!
